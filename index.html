About Me

My name is Sultan, 23 years old, and a passionate polyglot with a deep interest in languages and cultures,

currently learning my eighth language!

Since my school years, careful planning has shaped all of my time with discipline and enthusiasm!

I am dedicated to continuous learning and personal growth, and I love sharing my knowledge and strategies with others!

I believe that learning is a lifelong journey, and I am excited to help you reach your goals and discover your full potential!

I’m here to guide you and boost your skills!

How My Lessons Work

My lessons adapt to you – whether you are starting from zero or already at an advanced level! I will help you reach the next level quickly!

Focus on speaking: We practice speaking as much as possible to build your confidence and fluency!

Focused conversation practice & intensive speaking feedback as part of my learning packages:

I correct your mistakes directly and also provide additional written feedback for practice.

All materials in one place via Google Classroom:

You will receive an invitation to my virtual classroom on Google Classroom with all materials and exercises!

Polyglot advantage: If needed, I can explain concepts in multiple languages, including Azerbaijani, Turkish, Persian, English, German, Russian, and Chinese.
